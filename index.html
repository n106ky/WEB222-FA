#IntroToRummikub

Rummikub is a tile-based game, combining elements of the card game Rummy and mahjong. Every turn you can use your tiles or take a new one from the tile pool to make either Groups or Runs. Groups are formed by at least 3 tiles with the same number, but different colors. Runs consist in at least 3 tiles of the same color and with consecutive numbers. Unique for Rummikub is that players can work with the tiles that have already been played.


The game consists of 106 tiles in four different colors (black, red, blue, and yellow) and two jokers. Players start with a hand of tiles and take turns drawing and discarding tiles in order to form valid sets and runs. Players can manipulate existing melds on the table, rearranging tiles to create new combinations. A key rule is that players must always maintain a certain minimum value of points in their initial meld, which adds an additional layer of strategy to the game. The goal is to be the first to eliminate all the tiles from their rack. The players must puzzle out their best combinations and when more tiles are in play, the options becomes challenging and complex.
